Help

10 credits to spin, clicking Insert coin will add 1000 credits.

Auto spin will automatically spin for you, however it will stop spinning if you are able to Hold.

Holds are given if you get 2 of a kind.

Nudges not are yet implemented.

Scoring is work in progress but are as followed: One: 10 - Two: 20 - Three: 30 - Four: 40 - Five: 50 - Six: 60.

Known bugs

Due to the new code for spinning you will notice lagg when you match 3 when the 3rd reel is in Hold.
(This is because because matches are checked when the 3rd reel stops spinning, since it is in Hold you do not see this).

Holds are not reset if you choose not to use them. (Visual bug as this will not effect gameplay).

Click Spin! again while it is already running will make the game run an endless loop.

Credit no longer works (temp).
